Mobile users

This keyboard is designed for desktop computers. To type in lix wa on your smartphone, download a Unicode Myanmar keyboard and follow the instructions to switch your phone's keyboard. Then you don't need to use this page.

  • For Android, try MUA Keyboard or Gboard. MUA Keyboard is designed specifically for Myanmar languages, while Gboard is a multilingual keyboard from Google that just added Sgaw Karen and Pa'O Karen layouts in April 2019.
  • For iPhone, you might try Karen Keyboard.
